## SEC-03 verdict

**PASS.** Done-bar (D-04) met: (1) guardrail BLOCKED after ~$0.10 (403); (2) kill switch demonstrably stops owner-key spend (D-05 both switches); (3) email outcome empirically recorded (none — accepted). Spend ceiling honored (D-06: blocked at $0.1026, never reached the $0.25 abort).
